Cloudflare Email Routing

Enable customers to leverage their personal or professional domains to create personalized domains that forward emails to their preferred inbox.

Role Product Design Lead Timeline April 2020 - Current Blog Post Email Routing

Summary

Cloudflare Email Routing addresses a critical customer pain point: the complexity and cost of managing multiple custom domain mailboxes. Many users compromise security by using a single personal email for both sensitive logins (like banking) and spam-heavy web sign-ups. This product offers a simple, privacy-first alternative that empowers individuals, families, and small businesses to manage their online presences efficiently.

1

Configure Custom Domain Alias

The user defines their desired custom address on their domain (e.g., sales@mydomain.com) directly in the Cloudflare dashboard.

arrow_downward
2

Set Up Forwarding Target

The user inputs their preferred destination inbox (such as a personal Gmail or Outlook account) where messages should land.

arrow_downward
3

Verify & Auto-Configure

Cloudflare automatically provisions the required DNS records (MX/TXT) and validates destination ownership in real time with a quick confirmation link.

arrow_downward
4

Real-Time Intelligent Routing

Email arrives and is routed instantly. Cloudflare operates at the transport layer, modifying the SMTP envelope but preserving all security headers (SPF, DKIM) and keeping the email body completely intact.

From a design perspective, this setup achieves extreme simplicity for non-technical users while upholding a privacy-first approach. Because Cloudflare processes emails in real time without caching or storing them, users get the convenience of customized email addresses without sacrificing confidentiality or security.
