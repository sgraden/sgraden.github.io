Projects

IBM Cloud Databases Lead

Lead designer on the established IBM Cloud Databases portfolio. Covering Cloudant along with the revamped Compose suite of products such as PostgreSQL, Redis, MongoDB, ETC. Working to enhance the experience for these well known and loved products through user research and design exercises with the OM and development teams.

IBM Virtual Private Cloud

(March 2019) Designed the new set of infrastructure offerings. Focused primarily on our network offerings such as Security Groups, VPN, VPC, and Subnets, alongside some storage products like Custom Images. VPC is a complete technological overhaul of the entire infrastructure platform to provide our users with better customization and control at a faster speed.

Cloud Internet Services (Cloudflare)

(March 2018) Lead designer on IBM's partnership with Cloudflare. CIS is a suite of network services from a Load Balancer to DDoS protection to improve your applications performance and security.

Content Delivery Network

(October 2017) Design lead for IBM Cloud CDN. A tool to cache your websites data around the world, reducing bandwidth costs and increasing security.

Security Groups

(September 2017) Design lead for IBM Cloud Security Groups. Increase the security of your virtual servers by specifically controlling the inbound and outbound traffic. Limit what can connect to your server and how. (Built on the Softlayer portal)

Software Defined Networking

(September 2016) An earlier project when IBM was powered by OpenStack. This product enabled users to create virtual networks that helped segment their infrastructure for security and performance benefits.
