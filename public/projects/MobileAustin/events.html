  • Austin Gorilla Run
    Date: Jan 31, 2015
    Location: Mueller Browning Hangar
    Description:

    The Austin Gorilla Run is a charity fun run with a difference. Everyone who takes part is provided with a full gorilla costume, yours to keep with paid registration. This event helps raise funds for the Mountain Gorilla Conservation Fund, the international charity working the past 30 years to save the world's last remaining mountain gorillas. Lets keep Dian Fosseys dream alive for the next 30 years.
